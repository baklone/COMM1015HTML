2020-2023 Hong Kong Films

History of HK movie industry

When films from France was created in 1896, this industry was immediately spreaded and the first cinema was finally borned in Hong Kong in 1901. After 1935, Hong Kong stopped producing silent films and entered the era of sound films. While after the Second Sino-Jpanese War and Chinese Civil War, more people and captials came to Hong Kong as the political environment became more stable. For decades, Hong Kong film industry was the third largest following the United States cinema and Indian cinema. In the florescence, it can produced over 300 works in a year, which deserved to get the title of 'Eastern Hollywood'. However, in the middle of 1990s, Hollywood industry with the gimmick of CGI boosted and monopolised the market. With other external conditions, many investors and actors went to China. The genres of films became unitary to mainly crime films, losing the characteristics and creativity of production, thus, the decline in market. Until recently, more new directors tried their best to produce diversified films, in order to boost local market.

    • 2020s

    1. 一秒拳王 One second champion

    This is a local-produced film talking about a man borned with a special talent of predicitng the future one second. Yet, this talent is not being approbated by his family.
    When he is grown, his life is unsatisfactory due to the great loans and his wife leaving a hearing impaired son with him. Losing the ambition of life, he is then being invited to a boxing gym to contest. Until an accident happened and people confirmed about his superpower, he lost the qualifacations but luckily repaired the relationship with his son.
    This film shows that even a small talent should not be neglected
    and you should be prouded of it because it may change the future    .

    Trailer of One second champion


    2. 七人樂隊 Septet: the story of Hong Kong

    This is a film collection combined with 7 stories directed by 7 well-known directors.
    The film name 'Septet' represents the 7 directors incarnated as 7 musicians to play the unique music chapters of Hong Kong. Each story is filmed and presented in unique ways,
    presenting Hong Kong in different eras from 50s to 2000s.
    However, 70s is missing since Director Ng decided to quit the shooting due to health problem. Although a decade is not being recorded, the movie is still very special.
    One of the gimmics of this movie is that it is fully filmed with cine films,
    which aims to present the feel of old Hong Kong.

    2021s

    1. 智齒 Limbo

    'Limbo' is a film based on a China novel called 'Wisdom Tooth'.
    It is about a case cannot be solved by a new graduated police officer and a veteran officer,
    which then caused a serious of murder incidents. Later, the veteran officer met the girl
    who accidentally killed his wife and son and realised she got involved as a victim in the case.
    His innerside struggled a lot to whether save her or not. In order to perform the
    dirtiness, messiness and violence of the past Hong Kong was nowhere to hide,
    whole film used black and white as the main color tones.
    However, the props or settings are all created in details,
    which leads this film won the Golden Horse Award for Best Visual Effects.

    Trailer of Limbo


    2. 梅艷芳 Anita

    'Anita' is a biographical film about a very famous Cantopop star Anita Mui,
    which is to pay tribute to her legendary life. Overall, the film talks about her own life experience including love story, friendship and career life.
    From attractive yet detailed costumes to computer graphics (CG) environments,
    the effort behind this crew can be obviously seen
    as they did a lot of preparation and research to reappear the scenarios at that period of time.
    This recalls many people's collective memory of her well-known performance and songs,
    not only to cherish her moments of glory, but also her rough background and miserable life.

    Trailer of Anita


    2022s

    1. 正義迴廊 The Sparring Partner

    'The Sparring Partner' is a thriller film based on a true news in 2013,
    the 'Murder of Glory Chau and Moon Siu'.
    Everything started from a son calling the police of his parents disappeared
    after travelling to the Mainland China for two days.
    He and his older brother even created a Facebook page to search for their missing parents.
    But out of expectation, all these dramas were acted by the son and he was the real murderer.
    This film not just showing the ethic issue,
    but also aiming to present the microcosm of society in the court through atgonist characters. Moreover, this film aims to bring out a topic for audience
    for reflecting when everyone sympathized the innocent suspect:
    Bad guy cannot speak out the truth? Fool cannot kill people?

    Trailer of The Sparring Partner


    2. 飯氣攻心 Table for Six

    'Table for Six' is a commedy film that talks about a family of three brothers.
    The oldest brother's ex-girlfriend unexpectedly became his younger brother's girlfriend.
    In order to forget her, he decided to accept the new pursuer.
    While for the youngest brother, faced the crisis of break up.
    Under the complicated relationships, a normal dinner became a shamble.
    Facing this situation, everyone tried their best to maintain this family's harmony.
    This film is a commedy for Chinese New Year, yet, the hidden messages are deep:
    No matter what happened, the connection of family will never be damaged.
